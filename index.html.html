About Mods Switcher

🎯 What is Mods Switcher?

Mods Switcher is a lightweight utility designed specifically for GTA5 and RDR2 players who want hassle-free mod management. Instead of manually renaming files or moving folders, our tool provides a simple on/off switch for your entire mod collection.

🔧 How it Works

The tool works by temporarily renaming your mod files when you want to disable them, and restoring their original names when you want to enable them. This method is completely safe and reversible - your mods are never deleted or permanently modified.

🎮 Perfect for Online Play

Want to switch between modded single-player and clean online gameplay? Mods Switcher makes this transition seamless. Disable all mods with one click before going online, then re-enable them instantly when you're back to single-player.

💡 Why Choose Our Tool?

Unlike complex mod managers with dozens of features you don't need, Mods Switcher focuses on doing one thing perfectly: turning your mods on or off quickly and safely. No learning curve, no complicated setup - just install and use.

🛡️ Safety First

We understand how valuable your mod collection is. That's why our tool uses non-destructive methods to manage your mods. Even if something goes wrong, your original mod files remain intact and can be easily restored.

🚀 Future Updates

We're constantly working to improve Mods Switcher based on user feedback. Future updates may include individual mod selection, backup creation, and support for additional games. Stay tuned!
